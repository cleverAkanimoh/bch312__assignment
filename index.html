wilson's Disease

Wilson disease is a rare genetic condition that occurs when the body accumulates too much copper, especially in the liver and brain. The body needs a small amount of copper from food to stay healthy, but without treatment, Wilson disease can lead to high copper levels that cause life-threatening organ damage.
